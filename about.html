About Manish Academy

Built by teachers. Run like a classroom, not a factory.

We started in 2008 with one rented hall, 22 students and a simple promise: no assembly-line teaching. Sixteen years later, the promise hasn't changed — only the scale has.

MS

Mr. Manish Srivastava

Founder & Academic Director

From the Founder

“A coaching institute is judged by one thing — whether the average student did better than they would have on their own. Everything else is marketing.”

Before starting Manish Academy, I taught Mathematics at a government college for nine years. What I saw repeatedly was bright students crushed by poorly structured coaching — too much material, too little attention, and no one tracking if a concept actually landed. We built this institute to fix exactly that. We keep batches small, keep our senior faculty permanent, and keep parents informed with real data, not reassurances. That's it. That's the whole philosophy.

Our Mission

Make serious entrance preparation accessible without compromising on quality.

Tier-2 cities deserve the same depth of coaching that metros take for granted. Our mission is to deliver IIT-level Physics, AIIMS-level Biology and NDA-level discipline right here — at a fee structure local families can plan for.

Our Vision

Be the coaching institute students recommend to their younger siblings.

We measure success not by the number of admissions we get, but by how many of our selected students send their cousins, siblings and juniors to us next year. That number has grown every single year since 2012.

Our Journey

Sixteen years, one consistent idea.

Milestones we are quietly proud of — built batch by batch, not overnight.

2008

The first classroom

Started in a 1,200 sq ft rented hall in Civil Lines with 22 students in the inaugural JEE batch.

2012

First AIR in top 500

Aditya Ranjan cracked JEE Advanced with AIR 416 — validating the small-batch classroom model we had committed to.

2015

NEET & NDA wings launched

Added a dedicated Biology department and an NDA-Defence wing with permanent faculty.

2019

New campus, same promise

Moved to a 28,000 sq ft campus at Sector 14 — with lecture halls, a library, doubt rooms and a dedicated test centre.

2022

CUET programme introduced

Launched a focused CUET wing the very year the exam went national — first batch saw 71% students cross the 99 percentile.

2024

12,400+ students, 850+ selections

Crossed the 12,000 students milestone with a consistent 40%+ selection ratio across programmes.

Our Values

Four non-negotiables we refuse to compromise on.

01

Honesty in results

We publish only verified ranks. No inflated numbers. No screenshots of students who never attended.

02

Faculty stability

Our senior faculty have been with us 6–14 years. Students aren't rotated through unknowns.

03

Transparent fees

All-inclusive fee. No hidden material cost, test-series cost or “premium” module upsells.

04

Student wellbeing

Counsellors on campus. We treat burnout seriously; we stop piling material when a student is breaking.
